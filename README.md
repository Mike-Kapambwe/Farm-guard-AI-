# FarmGuard AI
"Know Your Farm. Find Your Support."

An AI-powered support navigator that helps small and mid-scale farmers in Africa understand and navigate the agricultural support system, with built-in crop and livestock disease diagnosis to guide them toward the right help at the right time.

## The Problem
Millions of small and mid-scale farmers face financial losses caused by crop diseases, livestock illness, drought, floods, and market disruptions. While governments and NGOs offer support programs, grants, subsidies, emergency relief, and technical assistance, many farmers are unaware these opportunities exist or struggle to understand eligibility requirements and application processes. As a result, farmers miss out on critical resources that could help them recover and protect their livelihoods.

## The Solution
FarmGuard AI helps farmers understand which agricultural support programs they may qualify for and guides them through the next steps. By asking personalized questions and translating complex eligibility rules into plain language, FarmGuard AI helps farmers discover relevant assistance, understand requirements, and access support more confidently. Crop and livestock health analysis is built directly into this journey, turning a diagnosis into a trigger that surfaces real support options.

## Core Features

### Support Navigation
Support Navigator (main feature) matching farmers to government and NGO programs they may qualify for
Eligibility Checker generating program-specific questions and a may be eligible assessment
Document preparation checklists for each program type
Local Expert Directory connecting farmers to agricultural officers and veterinary contacts
Farmer Community with a real WhatsApp group, success stories, and Farmer Q&A

### Farm Health Diagnosis
Crop Disease Analyzer and Livestock Health Advisor using photo, text, and voice input
Multiple issue detection per photo with severity and confidence ratings
Transparent AI reasoning explaining why each diagnosis was made
Realistic multi-action treatment plans matched to severity and urgency
Downloadable professional PDF diagnosis reports including the analyzed photo
Weather and Spray Guide with separate crop and livestock-specific guidance

### Personalization and Privacy
Farmer profile capturing farm size, experience, crops, animals, and current challenges
4-digit PIN protection with secure security-question recovery
Farm Assistant chat that remembers context and proactively guides farmers
Treatment and follow-up reminders via in-app banners and browser notifications

### Accessibility
Simple plain English throughout, designed for low-literacy users
Voice input for farmers who find typing difficult
8 supported interface languages
Offline access to history and prevention tips
Dark and light mode

## Responsible AI
Every result uses cautious, non-guaranteed language. Diagnoses always say "appears to be," never "is." Support results always say "may be eligible," never "you qualify." Every diagnosis includes a confidence level and a visible disclaimer. High severity cases are escalated with urgent expert referral via WhatsApp, SMS, and email. The AI never makes the final decision; the farmer does, supported by a qualified human expert for serious cases. Farmer data is never sent to or stored on any external server, staying only on the farmer's own device behind their PIN.
